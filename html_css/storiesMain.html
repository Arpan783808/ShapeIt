Stories

Storytelling is an effective method for mental health education because it engages individuals in a more personalized and meaningful way. It aids in illustrating various mental health difficulties and providing a better understanding of what it can be like to face these challenges. Also, it makes the content more relatable and interesting, which can assist to remove stigma and encourage comprehension.
