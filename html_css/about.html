OUR VISION

Our goal is to provide a thorough and easily accessible resource for anyone seeking help or information regarding mental health concerns. Our website will offer accurate and current information regarding various mental health issues, including causes, symptoms, and available treatments. Additionally, we will provide a self-assessment bot enabling users to evaluate their mental health and decide whether getting professional assistance would benefit them. The bot will offer tailored recommendations based on the user's answers, including details about nearby resources and how to get to them. Besides the self-evaluation tool, our website will provide support groups for individuals seeking a secure and encouraging environment to interact with like-minded people. These groups, facilitated by qualified professionals, will give people a forum to talk about their experiences, support one another, and get advice from mental health specialists. Our goal is to develop a website on which anyone looking for support and information on mental health issues can rely. Our mission is to provide individuals with the tools and resources they need to live their best lives, including the ability to take control of their mental health.

ABOUT US

Welcome to SHAPEIT, we're more than just a website; we're your one-stop shop for mental well-being. Like a family, we support your journey to a healthier, happier you.

Welcome to ShapeIt, your trusted online resource for mental health assessment and support. Our mission is to provide a safe and accessible platform where individuals can take tests to better understand their mental health. Whether you are seeking insights into ADHD, anxiety, or other mental health concerns, ShapeIt is here to help you on your journey towards well-being.

At ShapeIt, we believe that mental health is a crucial aspect of overall health. Our team is dedicated to creating reliable, user-friendly tools that empower individuals to gain clarity and seek the support they need. We offer a range of scientifically-backed tests designed to provide you with valuable insights into your mental health status.

We understand that taking the first step towards addressing mental health concerns can be daunting. That’s why we have designed our platform to be as intuitive and supportive as possible. Each test is accompanied by clear instructions and immediate feedback to help you interpret your results. Our goal is to make mental health assessments approachable and informative, fostering a community of understanding and support.

ShapeIt is more than just a website; it is a commitment to mental health awareness and support. We continuously work with mental health professionals to ensure our tests are accurate and up-to-date with the latest research. Our ultimate aim is to break down the stigma surrounding mental health and to promote a proactive approach to mental well-being.

Thank you for choosing ShapeIt as your partner in mental health. Together, we can shape a healthier, happier future.
